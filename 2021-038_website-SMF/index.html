Scope

Research Problem

At present, mushroom farming is conducted by the local farmers using their gut, feelings, experiances and using the naked eye vision. All these method are inefficient an unreliable methods leading to create inconsistancies.

The following operations are performed using the mentioned manual methods.

-->> Environmental factor monitoring & controlling.

-->> Harvest.

-->> Diesease spread identification.

These practices leads reduced mushroom yield production and inturn adveisly the farmers revenue.

Research Gap

The product is developed, where all the functions are found within one product.The environment monitoring component not only monitors the environment factors but it also provides a resource optimized recommendation. The harvest time detection component was not only to detect the harvest time but also to make the farmer notified how long the mushroom products are suitable for consumption. Disease detection component not only detects the disease unique to Sri lankan context and provides recommendations to stop the spread of disease. The yield prediction function uniqueness is generated by considering all the core factors of mushroom farming to give a monthly yield prediction to plan the farmers production and sales line and to make a good marketing plan.

Research Objectives

To minimized the cost for labour.

To minimized the production yield of mushrooms.

To etended the life span of mushrooms.

To increase mushroom product quality.

To prevent wastage of mushroom products.
